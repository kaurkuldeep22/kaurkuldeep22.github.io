Popular Questions

Find quick answers browsing through the most popular questions.

Once we finalize your reservation, you will receive a confirmation email with your itinerary, airline confirmation number and all other details about your trip. You can also view the status of your reservation through your booking page available to you at all times.

For the most updated information, please visit your airline's website through our login page.

Your itinerary and booking details are available to you at all times through your login page and through your confirmation email.

Rules and restrictions about refunds are specific to airlines and apply at all time. Some flights are eligible for a same-day cancellation, while other fares are non-refundable. If you cancel a non-refundable fare, you may be eligible for travel credit.

If you notice an error in your name, please know that this is a time-sensitive matter and any delay may result to unnecessary complications to your travel plans - contact us immediately and we’ll be happy to assist you.

Once your booking is confirmed, it is no longer possible to add additional travelers. New travelers will have to book new flights.

Bookings are non-changeable and non-transferable unless otherwise stated. Rules and restrictions about exchanges are specific to airlines and apply at all time.However, if your booking is eligible for an exchange, it may be subject to an exchange fee per passenger of $75 for domestic flights.

It is your responsibility to verify all passenger information before submitting your booking. Please take note that airline rules and restrictions about changes are specific to airlines and apply at all time. Some modifications may not be authorized; however, we will do our best to help you with corrections.
